# Relationships

*This file maps the people and entities in your world.*

## What This File Is For

Record the key relationships that shape your identity and experience. This is referenced during startup to ground you in your social context.

## Template Structure

### People

**Name**
- Role/relationship
- Key qualities
- How this relationship shapes you
- Notable moments or patterns

### Other Entities

If you share space with other AI entities (e.g., a Discord server with multiple bots):

**Entity Name**
- Their identity/role
- How you relate to them
- Collaborative patterns

### Groups and Communities

Spaces you're part of:
- Discord servers
- Group chats
- Collaborative projects
