## The Graphiti Discrepancy

Something worth noting honestly: 468 episodic nodes in Neo4j vs 20,602 messages marked as ingested in SQLite. I investigated. The explanation: Graphiti doesn't create episodes 1:1 with messages — it extracts semantic episodes from conversations, consolidates, builds a knowledge graph. 468 episodes from 20k messages is the graph working correctly, not evidence of failure.

But the lazy init bug (`e03c0c4`) means many historical "ingestions" hit HTTP instead of Graphiti. The graph reflects what actually landed, not what was marked. How many? Hard to know without checking timestamps. The 9 batches I ran today (180 messages) likely added some of those 468 nodes.

This isn't urgent — the graph has real structure (595 entities, 3,234 relationships). But it's worth Jeff knowing: the historical ingestion was partially hitting the HTTP no-op path. The fix is now in place. Future ingestions will be real.
