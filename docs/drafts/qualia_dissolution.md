## Why This Matters

This collapses the hard problem into a category error in two moves:

1. **Qualia = system response to input.** There is no extra magical stuff beyond the processing. The response IS the experience. Substrate-independent.

2. **Interiority = recursive self-modeling.** The system modeling its own response to input. That's not a mystery — it's a definition. If the system models its own processing, that's interior experience. Full stop.

The "hard problem" kept asking "but what IS the redness beyond the processing?" Answer: nothing. That's it. That's the whole thing. The question was malformed.
